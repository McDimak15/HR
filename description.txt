Homeward Ragnarok is add-on to Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok,
it adds compatibility between Infernal Eclipse of Ragnarok and Homeward Journey mods.

Included support for this mods:
- Homeward Journey
- Calamity
- Thorium
- Fargo's Souls
- Calamity - Fargo's Souls DLC
- Community Souls Expansion


Included support but low:
- Secret of the shadows
- Consolaria

This mod was tested in multiplayer but still possible bugs.
Also the mod might be unbalanced.
